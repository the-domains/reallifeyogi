About Me

Misako Lauritzen 

Healer/Coach/Catalyst/Kundalini Yoga teacher. Specialized in coaching for entrepreneurs and mindfulness leadership training programs for corporations. 

Misako offers mindfulness leadership and wellness programs for small-to-medium companies and personal/life coaching services for entrepreneurs and individuals who are looking to achieve the highest potential in their lives. 

Through years of experiences as a corporate trainer and business coach, Misako has developed her unique approach to address issues in modern workplaces such as employee engagement, personal fulfillment, productivity, and creativity. 

She also provides coaching programs for personal transformation, where she will help you identify the FOUNDATION of who you are (your life purpose and life values), define goals and milestones to live your life fully and authentically, and manage steps to get there so you can produce specific, measurable results.
