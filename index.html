What is Real Life
Yogi?

Yoga is "jugit" in Sanskrit, which

means "to unite."


Therefore, the

purpose of
yoga is to unite

polarities

(good
and bad, light
and darkness,
joy
and
sadness,
and
etc...)
and
achieve balance.
